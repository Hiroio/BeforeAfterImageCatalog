# Before/After Slider

So, you have this code. What now? Here is your 4-step survival guide:

# 1. Open Xcode
Launch that giant blue app on your Mac. You know the one.

# 2. Press Play
Click the big **▶ (Play)** button in the top left corner. 
*(Or just press `Cmd + R` if you want to look like a pro).*

# 3. Wait a bit
Wait for the iPhone simulator to wake up. Give it a second.

# 4. Voilà!
Grab the slider with your mouse and drag it. Look at you, you're a developer now!
